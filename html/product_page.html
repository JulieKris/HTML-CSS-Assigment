Godzilla: King of the monsters

2019 Directed by Michael Dougherty

When Emma Russell, a palaeontologist working for the titan-hunting organization monarch, and her daughter is kidnapped by an eco-terrorist, the rest of Monarch are left to rescue them with the help of Godzilla. Things escalate as Emma’s true intentions are revealed and even more gigantic monsters are introduced into the mix.

Starring: Kyle Chandler, Vera Farmiga, Millie Bobby Brown, Bradley Whitford, Sally Hawkins, Charles Dance, Thomas Middleditch, Aisha Hinds, O’shea Jackson Jr., David Strathairn, Ken Watanabe, Zhang Ziyi.
Genres: Science fiction, Action
