our concept

At SquareEyes we want to offer you the ability to discover and support your favorite film creators directly, to see their works in the best quality and to share your joys with others. Films bring people together, whether at the cinema, or at home with friends and family. Because we want movies to keep bringing people together, we put an emphasis on social features. You can rate and review your films and see your friends activities as well.
